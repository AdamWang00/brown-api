Engineering & Friedman Cafes

Engineering Cafe

Regular Hours: Weekdays 8:00am-5:00pm

Conveniently located in the School of Engineering Building. Some of the featured items include Nitro Iced Coffee, Expressos, Lattes, Hot Lunch Sandwiches, snacks, grab-&-go goodies, as well as freshly baked goods made by the Brown Bakeshop to help fuel your studies.

Friedman Cafe

Regular Hours: Monday – Thursday  8:30am – 12:00 am; Friday  8:30am – 6:00pm; Saturday  10:00am – 10:00pm; Sunday  10:00am – 12:00am.

The Friedman Cafe, in the lobby of the SciLi, is at the center of the Friedman Study Center, a 24-hour study space in the center of campus.  The cafe is a convenient spot for Fair Trade coffee, grab & go sandwiches, salad and house-made parfait and vegetable cups, as well as freshly baked goods from the Brown Bakeshop.

Rock & Poppy's Cafes

Rock Cafe

Regular Hours: Daily 10:00am-8:00pm

Newly renovated, the Rock Cafe features fairly-traded coffee, Tazo teas, and a wide selection of bottled beverages. Snack on cereal, oatmeal, a daily selection of freshly made salads and sandwiches, as well as, baked goods made by the Brown Bakeshop.

Poppy's Cafe

Regular Hours: Daily 10:00am-8:00pm

Fuel your body and mind with foods that recharge, rebuild, replenish and rejoice. Located in the lobby of the Jonathan Nelson Fitness Center & Katherine Moran Coleman Aquatics Center, Poppy’s is a convenient place to nourish your body with customizable fresh fruit and juice blends. Whether you’re an athlete coming for your daily exercise, or want to grab a bite on your way to work, Poppy’s has a wide selection of tasty, nutrient-dense foods ready for you.

Eat Right — For You

Do you care about whether your food was grown locally or raised humanely? Or are you more interested in protein vs. carbs?

Whatever your priorities, we can help you chew the right thing. At Bon Appétit, we’re working toward “food service for a sustainable future.” How we define that is a mouthful, but the bottom line is that we strive to cook delicious food that’s good for you, the animals, the workers, the community, and the Earth — because they’re all connected. And we’re proud to be the first food service company to tackle many important issues in these areas.

We’re here to support you by giving the information you need to choose wisely. Visit our Wellness page for a feast of information about the roles our from-scratch cooking, nutritional approach, local sourcing, and other sustainability programs can play on your menu.

Meal Options for Isolated Students

Brown Dining offers two meal options for every isolated student with flu-like symptoms. Student with flu-like symptoms must register on fluweb. You can then choose one of these two meal options available from Brown Dining via pickup:

  1. Pickup of Individual Meals at Dining Halls by Flu Buddy: Flu Buddies may pick up one takeout meal during each meal period (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner) at either the Refectory or the Verney-Woolley dining hall. Flu Buddies are required to sign in at the cashier’s station. Sharpe Refectory: Monday – Saturday 7:30 am – 7:30 pm •  Sunday 10:30 am – 7:30 pm / Verney Woolley: Weekdays, Breakfast 7:30 am – 11:00 am, Lunch 11:00 am – 2 pm,  Dinner 4:30 pm – 7:30 pm
  2. Pickup of “Food Kit” by Flu Buddy: Food Kits may be picked up at the Receiving area of the Refectory (corner of George and Thayer, loading dock entrance; ring doorbell for assistance if door is locked). A Food Kit is a 5 day supply of food. One kit per student. Pickup Hours of Operation: Monday – Saturday 8:00 am – 7:00 pm • Sunday 11:00 am – 3:00 pm

Faculty Club

The Brown Faculty Club provides a year-round, private and attractive gathering place in the heart of the Brown University campus that is perfect for dining or for entertaining associates, friends, and family.

The Club’s well-appointed, flexible interior provides suitable facilities for private parties, wedding receptions, banquets and departmental meetings. Lunch and dinner service is also offered.
